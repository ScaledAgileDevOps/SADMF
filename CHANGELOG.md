# CHANGELOG

## 2026-03-04

### New Content

- **[Change Request Lead Time](/metrics/change-request-lead-time/)** — New metric page. Measures elapsed days between change record creation and CRAP approval. Minimum target: 5 calendar days. Excellence target: 30 calendar days. Includes Planning Excellence commendation and Sustained Foresight Award for teams achieving the 30-day standard.

### Updated Content

- **[Change Adjudication Convening](/release-convoy/ceremonies/change-adjudication-convening/)** — Added *Integrated Record and Signature Protocol* section. Covers the enterprise change management platform record requirement, automatic physical form generation on Code Inspection open, minimum five-signature collection (including two change governance representatives), signature verification against records on file, re-approval loop on verification failure, and physical archive storage as the authoritative approval record.

- **[Change Rejection or Acceptance Party](/roles/governance/change-rejection-or-acceptance-party/)** — Added *Pre-Submission Approval Requirements* section. Clarifies the two-tier governance structure: five pre-submission signatures must be collected and verified before a change may appear on the Convening agenda; the CRAP's unanimous ballot is a separate and subsequent gate.
